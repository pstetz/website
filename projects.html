Click anywhere below! (Do not recommend on phone)

Hold down shift to keep the planet stationary and the longer you click down the bigger the planet gets. Refresh to start over again. There is a maximum of 8 planets. There's a maximum speed limit and the gravitational force diminishes close to each horizon so that the behavior isn't too buggy. Movement can be a bit jumpy when a huge object meets a really small object.

Looking on the bright side, try making a really big stationary planet and put much smaller planets inside it. Very interesting behavior. Makes me think what the world would look like if there were discrete time intervals and a maximum speed limit
